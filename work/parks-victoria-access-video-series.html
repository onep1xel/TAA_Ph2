The Access Agency started in it's original form in 2016.

For 20 years we've worked in digital agencies on some iconic brands. Now, we're taking that experience, our knowledge of Australian Standards on Access and our deep connections within the disability community, to create a unique consultancy. We our aim to be a world-class partner for the visitor economy.
